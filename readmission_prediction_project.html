Hospital Readmission Risk Prediction

Machine Learning Model for Healthcare Analytics

A comprehensive predictive analytics solution designed to identify patients at high risk of hospital readmission within 30 days. This project leverages advanced machine learning algorithms and healthcare data to provide actionable insights for healthcare providers, ultimately improving patient outcomes while reducing healthcare costs.

Project Overview

Hospital readmissions are a critical challenge in healthcare, affecting both patient outcomes and healthcare costs. This project develops a sophisticated machine learning model that analyzes patient demographics, medical history, clinical indicators, and treatment patterns to predict the likelihood of readmission within 30 days of discharge.
